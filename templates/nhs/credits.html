Credit Requirements

Annual Requirements

  • 25 total service hours/credits per academic year
  • At least 10 hours must be in community service
  • At least 5 hours must be in school service or tutoring
  • The remaining 10 hours can be in any approved category

Credit Values

  • 1 hour of service = 1 credit
  • Leadership roles may qualify for additional credits
  • Organizing events may earn up to 1.5x normal credits
