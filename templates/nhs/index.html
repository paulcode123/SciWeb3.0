About Our NHS Chapter

Our National Honor Society chapter recognizes students who demonstrate excellence in scholarship, service, leadership, and character. NHS members are committed to making a positive impact in both our school and the broader community.

Through a variety of service projects, tutoring initiatives, and leadership opportunities, we strive to foster personal growth and build a stronger, more supportive academic environment for all students.

Our Four Pillars

Scholarship

Maintaining a minimum 3.5 GPA and showing dedication to learning and academic growth.

Service

Contributing time and energy to improve the school and community through volunteer activities.

Leadership

Demonstrating initiative, positive influence, and the ability to inspire and guide others.

Character

Exhibiting qualities such as integrity, respect, and ethical behavior in all aspects of life.
