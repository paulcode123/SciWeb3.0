Academic Excellence for All

SciWeb uses AI to provide structured learning, organizes your academic journey, and connects you with a community of learners.

Get Started Learn More

Our Mission

To build a platform that makes academic excellence universally accessible.

Our Values

Excellence

SciWeb uses structured AI to provide the highest quality of support for students.

Accessibility

SciWeb is a free, open-source, and community-driven platform that is accessible to all students.

Discovery

SciWeb guides students to derive concepts from within their own knowledge, and to discover new connections between concepts.

Connection

SciWeb fosters community within academic circles and enables the seamless sharing of ideas and resources.

Our Journey

2023

The Beginning

SciWeb was founded by 3 Bronx Science students who were frustrated with the lack of academic resources available to them.

2024

Development Phase

After 2 rejected club applications, the team continued to develop the platform, writing 5 million lines of code over 1000+ hours.

2025

The Launch

SciWeb was finally launched as a state-of-the-art academic platform with almost 15 collaborators over 3 departments.

Today

Growing Impact

We've received many endorsements from students, faculty, and administrators as we continue to expand our impact.

Our Philosophy

We believe that during the learning process, the brain organizes new information into a web of interconnected concepts, and that developing this web is the key to true understanding.

Rigorous Visualization

SciWeb uses concept maps to represent the students present understanding of the topic. We believe that our understanding of a topic is not stored as words and sentences but rather as patchworks of ideas. When we reconstruct and visualize these ideas, we can better understand the connections between them.

Integrative Learning

SciWeb personalizes your learning journey by probing your knowledge and expanding familiar ideas into new ones.

Full Customization

SciWeb has a plethora of features, tools, and options to suit any student's needs and learning style. All of them are neatly organized and represented on our website.

Our Innovations

Structured AI

SciWeb exploits structured, decentralized AI to combine the accessibility of AI with the precision and organization of human instruction and guidance.

Motivational Design

SciWeb plays into the motivational and reward structures of the human brain by linking everything you do to your personal goals and aspirations.
